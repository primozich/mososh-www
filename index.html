Chronicles of Herenvale

Chronicles of Herenvale is a fantasy adventure game that you can play on your phone or through any web browser. The game is availabe through popular social networks like Facebook and Kongregate as well as a stand-alone site.

Version 1.0 launched at the end of 2010 on a social network called hi5. Since then the game has expanded to three more social networks, play.herenvale.com as well as iOS and android.

Cyberpunks vs Syndicates

Explore a dystopian future completing missions while you build and configure your army for massive assaults with other players.

Cyberpunks vs Syndicates launched in the summer of 2013 and is currently available on Facebook, Kongregate and Armor Games.
